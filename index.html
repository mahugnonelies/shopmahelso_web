ShopMahelso Inventory

Manage your products, sales and stock in real time with a modern and intuitive interface.

Who is ShopMahelso for?

The application is designed for small and medium-sized businesses that need a simple and reliable way to track their inventory.

Physical Stores

Fashion, cosmetics, accessories, hardware… centralize products, barcodes, stock entries and exits.

Online Sellers

Manage a single stock for online orders and in-store sales. Avoid stock-outs and overselling.

Warehouses & Storage

Track movements, employee activity, alerts and detailed reports to manage your volume efficiently.

Key Features

Everything you need to stay in control of your inventory.

Advanced Product Management

Create products with SKU, variants, purchase price, selling price and photos. Quickly filter by category, shop or stock level.

One Owner & Multiple Employees

Create your shop as the owner. Invite employees with a unique shop code and assign permissions.

Stock In & Out

Track every movement (purchase, sale, correction, loss…) with full history and responsible employee.

Sales Validation

Order system with owner validation. Reduce errors and get better visibility on profit margins.

Reports & Analytics

Clear dashboards: sales by day, product, employee or shop. Export available for accounting analysis.

Low Stock Alerts

Notifications and reports for low stock levels. Adjust thresholds per product.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few answers to common questions.

Is ShopMahelso suitable for small shops?

Yes. It was designed for small and medium-sized businesses. You can start with one shop and a few users, then grow.

Can I manage multiple shops?

Yes. You can create multiple shops using different emails to keep inventory separated.

Can my employees use the app?

Yes. You can invite your employees by sharing your shop’s unique code. This code is confidential and must not be shared publicly.

Which devices are supported?

The app works on all mobile devices. You can manage inventory from anywhere.

Can I request a custom app for my shop?
